Common Questions

Buyer FAQ

Do I need to be pre-approved before I start looking?

It's not required, but we strongly recommend it. Pre-approval gives you a clear budget and shows sellers you're a serious buyer — especially important in a competitive Idaho market.

How much does it cost to use a buyer's agent?

In most transactions, the seller pays both agents' commissions, so buyer representation through CWRE Idaho typically costs you nothing out of pocket. We'll explain the current structure clearly upfront.

How long does it take to buy a home in Idaho?

From starting your search to closing, the process typically takes 30–90 days depending on market conditions, your flexibility, and financing. We'll give you a realistic timeline from day one.

What areas do you cover?

We serve 13 communities across Southwest Idaho — including Boise, Eagle, Meridian, Nampa, Caldwell, Star, Kuna, Emmett, Middleton, Mountain Home, New Plymouth, Melba, Marsing, and Payette.
