Living in Kuna

Affordable South Treasure Valley Living

Kuna sits in the southern end of Ada County, just 18 miles southwest of Boise via I-84, and offers buyers an affordable alternative to the higher-priced north valley communities. It's gained a reputation as a hidden gem — quiet, family-friendly, with good schools, and more land for your dollar than almost anywhere else in Ada County.

The Snake River Birds of Prey National Conservation Area is practically in Kuna's backyard, offering dramatic canyon views, hiking, and birding. The city has grown substantially but retained its close-knit community character.

Why Buyers Choose Kuna

  • One of the most affordable home prices in Ada County
  • Quiet, established neighborhoods with good community feel
  • Snake River Birds of Prey NCA — world-class raptor habitat
  • Ada County schools — Kuna School District
  • Easy I-84 access to Boise, Nampa, and Meridian
  • More space and acreage options than north valley communities

Schools

  • Kuna School District
  • Kuna High School, Kuna Middle School
  • Multiple elementary schools serving growing neighborhoods
