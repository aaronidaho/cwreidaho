Living in Meridian

Idaho's Fastest-Growing City

Meridian is the second-largest city in Idaho and has been one of the fastest-growing cities in the entire United States for over a decade. What draws people here? A combination of excellent schools, modern shopping and dining at The Village at Meridian, abundant new construction in well-planned neighborhoods, and easy freeway access to Boise and the broader Treasure Valley.

Meridian offers something for everyone — first-time buyers can find more affordable entry-level homes than Boise or Eagle, while move-up buyers have access to luxury new construction in prestigious master-planned communities.

Meridian Neighborhoods

Bridgetower

Upscale gated community with resort amenities, larger lots, and proximity to top Meridian schools.

Tuscany

Beautiful master-planned community with pools, parks, and a range of price points from starter to luxury.

Paramount

One of North Meridian's most popular communities — great amenities, top schools, and strong resale values.

Development in North Meridian

Ongoing new construction with the latest floor plans, energy efficiency, and modern finishes.

Meridian Schools

Meridian is served by West Ada School District, one of Idaho's largest and highest-rated public school districts. The district consistently earns strong academic ratings and has excellent athletic and extracurricular programs.

  • West Ada School District (formerly Meridian Joint School District)
  • Meridian High School, Rocky Mountain High School, Heritage High School
  • Multiple highly-rated elementary and middle schools
  • Charter options including Treasure Valley Classical Academy

Shopping, Dining & Amenities

  • The Village at Meridian — premier outdoor shopping, dining, and entertainment
  • Meridian Speedway, TopGolf, and major entertainment venues
  • Extensive parks, splash pads, and recreation centers
  • St. Luke's Meridian Medical Center — major hospital campus
