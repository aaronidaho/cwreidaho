My Story

Idaho Real Estate, Done Right

I grew up watching Idaho grow — from quiet farming communities to one of the fastest-growing states in the nation. That growth is exciting, but it also means buyers and sellers need an agent who understands the land, the people, and the market at a deeper level than a quick Google search can provide.

That's what CWRE Idaho is built on. With over 15 years of experience serving buyers and sellers across the Treasure Valley and Southwest Idaho, I've helped hundreds of families find their place in this beautiful state — whether that's a first home in Meridian, a rural retreat near Emmett, or a long-awaited upgrade in Eagle.

My approach is simple: I listen first. Every client has a unique situation, unique goals, and a unique timeline. I'm not here to push you into a deal — I'm here to make sure the deal you make is the right one for you.
